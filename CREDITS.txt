OrbVista - Credits & Licenses
============================================================

OrbVista is a comprehensive 3D visualization and analysis 
suite for quantum chemical data, supporting MO, ESP, and 
Spin-Density analysis with advanced export capabilities.

This application is provided as open-source software. 
It bundles several third-party libraries, each governed 
by their own licenses:

CORE QUANTUM CHEMISTRY & DATA:
------------------------------------------------------------
1. PySCF (Apache License 2.0)
   https://pyscf.org
   (Used for SCF, DFT, and GTO orbital calculations)

2. cclib (BSD 3-Clause License)
   https://github.io
   (Used for parsing quantum chemical log files)

3. h5py (BSD License)
   https://h5py.org
   (Used for efficient HDF5 data management)

4. NumPy & SciPy (BSD License)
   https://numpy.org | https://scipy.org
